Parklife Festival - Heaton Park, Manchester, UK

Hosted in the lovely city of Manchecter, Parklife festival is manchesters biggest festival having been host in the past to big names from both the UK scene and America. Having been delayed in 2020 due to the pandemic, it returned in 2021 and was said to have a capacity of around 80,000 people. Big name artists such as Megan Thee Stallion, Young Thug and Migos all performed and were among some of the bigest crowd pullers there as people travelled from all over England just see them.

Leed Festival - Bramham Park, Leeds, UK

Standing as one of the biggest festivals in England, Leeds festival started in 1999 due to increasing demand and has been running for over 20 years now. Leeds festival has a wide range of artists from various genres of music, ranging from fan favourites like Liam Callagher to home grown grime talent such as Fredo. Leeds festival simultaneously takes place at the same time as Reading Festival. Whilst Leeds festival is the little sibling to Reading Festival, its popularity has grown tremendously as 105,000 people attended in 2019. Several hip-hop artists such as E minem, 50 Cent and Jay-Z have appeared over the years.

Glastonbury Festival - Somerset, UK

This top charting five-day festival is world renowned and officialy stands as the UK's largest festival. Attendees fly in from all over the world just experience this grand spectacle. Boasting an attendance of a staggering 203,000, Glastonbury is a event to definitely experience at least once in your life. Aside from music, there are also other forms of entertainment such as comedy, dance and cabaret. London artist Stormzy headlined the main stage alondisde Mile Cyrus and The Killers.
